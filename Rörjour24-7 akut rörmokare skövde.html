Core Google Ads & Conversion Issues

  • Local Intent Signal Weakness: Landing page does not strongly reinforce Skövde-specific positioning above the fold.

  • Conversion Friction: Large booking form competes with the primary “call now” action, increasing hesitation in emergency situations.
  • Geo Targeting Risk: Multiple national city pages are visible (Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö, etc.), indicating possible budget dilution.
  • Campaign Structure Risk: Emergency and general VVS services likely blended within the same campaign structure, reducing intent clarity.
