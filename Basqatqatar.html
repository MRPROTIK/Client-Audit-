Audit Breakdown

One

Non-SEO-friendly URLs (.php + parameters). Pages use /index.php, /contactus.php, services.php?gid=... style URLs. This hurts readability, consistency, and long-term SEO scaling.

Two

Crawl / verification interruptions (risk). Some requests may trigger verification/anti-bot behavior. If search engines face this, indexing and visibility can be impacted—especially for high-intent pages like Contact.

Three

On-page trust & relevance signals need alignment. Titles/search preview messaging may not consistently reflect core services, and contact details appear inconsistent across locations—both can reduce trust and lead conversions.
